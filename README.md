## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary trends, and employee satisfaction. The dashboard helps organizations identify key attrition drivers and make data-driven HR decisions.

## Business Insights

* Overtime is a major contributor to employee attrition.
* Certain job roles experience significantly higher turnover.
* Salary and job satisfaction influence employee retention.
* Business travel frequency impacts attrition behavior.
* Workforce demographics reveal important retention patterns.
